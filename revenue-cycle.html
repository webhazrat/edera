Revenue Cycle

National Coordination Center’s (NCC’s) vision and strategy include providing world-class end-to-end revenue cycle support to our clients. From best practice advisory and consultancy services to day-to-day business operations support, we cover the full spectrum of revenue cycle services.

Service Offerings

  • Registration/Scheduling
  • Establish Patient Record to Initiate Clinical Events
  • Preauthorization
  • Care Coordination
  • Order/Charge Reconciliation
  • Clinical Documentation Review and Accuracy
  • HIM Coding (Acute and Ambulatory)
  • Charge Services
  • Billing
  • Account Management
  • Accounts Receivable (AR) Follow-Up
  • Review Claim Edits and Denials to Improve Upstream Clinical Processes

Using the NCC Fusion Methodology framework, we leverage our processes and network of IBPAs to deliver world-class end-to-end revenue cycle support.

We provide CDRC support to assure a collaborative operation and bridge communication between clinicians and the patient accounting team. Our CDRC optimization offerings help clients:

  • Improve clinical documentation quality
  • Achieve desired revenue cycle key performance indicators (KPIs)

NCC creates frameworks and leverages its expertise across our RCO team to include:

  • Data Entry
  • Charge Services
  • Coding
  • Revenue Utilization Review
  • Registration/Scheduling
  • Account Management
  • Billing/Reporting

To assure upstream clinical processes are generating accurate and timely revenue, we provide:

  • Preparation and development of clinical (charge) process guides
  • Training assistance prior to the Go-Live event on clinical (charge) process guides
  • Daily reconciliation process assistance at the department level during the Go-Live period
  • Daily dashboard reporting
