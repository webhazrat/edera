Inspiring Innovation.
Catalyzing Change.

Edera L3C is a mission-focused, low-profit health care consulting firm based in the District of Columbia. As the only firm in the United States to have successfully executed the unique strategy of combining industry experts with full-time creative, technology, and management consulting resources, we flex to bring you the expertise and capabilities needed to solve your most complex real-world challenges. With our holistic human-centered approach, best-in-class consulting protocols, industry-specific best practices, bespoke technological tools, and commitment to delivery excellence, Edera creates value that lasts.

Why We Exist

Our Mission

With a heighted focus on delivery excellence and social impact, Edera partners with clients to ideate, build, implement and sustain innovative, human-centric business solutions.

Our Purpose

Edera exists to help organizations build better businesses and effect positive change in society.

What it Means to Be a Low Profit LLC

Edera is mission-driven and operates with a purpose-before-profit mindset. Profits beyond the sustainability goals of our organizations are reinvested into our communities (e.g., community improvement initiatives) and/or our clients (e.g., indirect rate reinvestment or alignment).
