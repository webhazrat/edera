Purpose Before Profit

As a Low-Profit Limited Liability Company (L3C), Edera exists to help organizations build better businesses and effect positive change in society. Profits beyond the sustainability goals of our organizations are reinvested into our communities (e.g., community improvement initiatives) and/or our clients (e.g., indirect rate reinvestment or alignment).
